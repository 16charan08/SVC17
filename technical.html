Chapter BIOMANIA

In this event participants will be divided into a group of 4. Each group will be given questions (including questing from all branches life sciences). On solving these questions participants will get a hint to enter the next level. Only 3 teams will be allowed to the next level, where only one will qualify. The finalists will have to solve questions to get to a place or a person. The one coming arriving first to the place or person will win.

Chapter code zeal

coding event.

Round1

This is an aptitude, C, Java, General Knowledge questions based quiz. Duration: 40-60 min..

Round2

The persons qualified in first round will go to 2nd round. The number of teams qualify for the second round depends on number of participants. In this round the participants will be given 3 problem statement and have to solve with their logic using any one of the three programming languages C, Java, Python. Duration: 90 min (each question 30 min)

EQUERY 2.0

This is a general quiz for 1st years and a C, General Knowledge(technical) quiz for other than 1st years. So two separate papers. Duration: 60-90 min.

Chapter Equiz

A quick fun quiz, to test your knowledge and competence in electronics and associated topics. Questions will be asked based on the basic concepts of electronics. The best thing is if you know the answer you’ll get points and if you don’t you’ll learn something out of it. So get ready to participate in the “E-Quiz”.

Chapter Designer den

War Of Websites: War Of Websites is a good initiative to exhibit your innovative talents. In this competition an output of the website and related information will be disclosed and the website must be created accordingly in the stipulated time and winners will be awarded .
Master Of Poster: Master of poster provides an opportunity to exhibit your creative and technical skills. In this competition a topic will be given to the participants and posters to be designed accordingly in the given stipulated time and talent will be certified.

Chapter Hackthon

Hackathon brings the students an opportunity to enhance their coding skills. It provides the students with an opportunity to make virtual existing robots like natasha on hike. The participants will be given training on virtual existing robots and later a competition is conducted based on the given training and winners will be awarded accordingly.
